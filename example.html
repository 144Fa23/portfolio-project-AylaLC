Lesson Purpose

In my business classes, my lessons will be centered around hands-on experiences for my students. For example, actively creating ads in Marketing, running a school-wide small business in Entrepreneurship, and job shadowing in Personal Finance. The purpose of these hands-on lessons is to ensure that my students are building transferable skills that they can use in their lives after high school. Additionally, many of my lessons will be created with the intention of helping my students create goals and plans for their futures
