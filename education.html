My Education Philosophy

It is my belief that all students should have access to a qualilty education. That way, they can build the necessary skills to be successful in life after high school. To help my learners grow as people and as students, I will:

  • Allow them to exercise their own thoughts and opinions
  • Aid them in identifying their goals for their futures
  • Guide them towards a path that will help them reach their goals
  • Showcase opportunities that will be open to them in the future
  • Implement project-based learning activities into my lessons that will enable my learners to gain hands-on experience
